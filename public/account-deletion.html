iSkiLog Account Deletion Request

How to Request Account Deletion

If you would like to permanently delete your iSkiLog account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

Option 1: Request by Email

Send an email to:

damir.filaretov@gmail.com

From the email address associated with your iSkiLog account.

Include the subject line:

Account Deletion Request

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User account information including email address
  • All seasons
  • All logged sets
  • All event specific data
  • All personal notes
  • Associated timestamps

Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • All user data is permanently removed from the active database.
  • Backup systems may retain encrypted copies for up to 30 days for security and recovery purposes.
  • After this period, all data is automatically purged.

We do not sell, share, or retain deleted data for marketing purposes.

Alternative: Delete Within the App

If a Delete Account feature becomes available inside the app, users may delete their account directly from Settings.
